Love Hedgehogs!

Hedgehogs are adorable little mammals that snuffle happily through the grass.

But don't let their cuteness fool you - their bodies are covered in spines that become very stiff and sharp when they curl up out of fear!

Hedgehogs have

  • slug sniffers
  • snaildars
  • froth dispensaries
  • sightbeans
  • grubby grabbers
  • velveteen tums
  • stiffy stabbies
  • curlable posteriors
